A full-fledged isometric action RPG that delves into the lore of Old World Frontier, a real-time strategy business sim that takes place in an alternate universe in a distant post apocalyptic future of Earth

The prison enclosure that has kept generations of your family safe from outsiders has been breached. You are tasked to venture outside the boundary of your home to seek for a new home, and to look for answers.

Utilizing Old World Frontier's skill system, players can have meaningful character builds with intertwined skills, perk rewards, etc.
